# 🤝 Code of conduct

We follow the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
in spirit. The short version:

- 🙌 Be kind. Assume good intent.
- 🧠 Disagree on technical merit, not on the person.
- 🪴 Welcome newcomers. Explain context that's obvious to long-time contributors.
- 🚫 No harassment, slurs, or doxxing in issues, PRs, discussions, or any
  community channel.

Maintainers may remove comments, close PRs, or block contributors who
violate this in any forum.

## 📮 Reporting

Email **pr@conscience.technology** with the subject prefix
`[Quad conduct]`, or message any maintainer privately. Reports stay
confidential; we'll respond within 7 days.
